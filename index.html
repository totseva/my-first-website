Hi, I’m Marina Totseva — a Japanese philology graduate, self-taught programmer, and UX/UI explorer.

Fluent in seven languages and deeply curious about the world, I’m fascinated by how psychology and culture shape human experience.

This curiosity drives my work in frontend development and UX/UI design, where I blend user experience, cultural insight, and code to craft intuitive, engaging, and human-centered digital spaces.

I created C³X — Creativity, Code & Culture in UX — to explore how the human mind leaves its mark on design. Websites, languages, and interfaces each reflect the people who interact with them.

Welcome to CCC Design Lab, where creativity, culture, and code intersect to bring digital experiences to life.
